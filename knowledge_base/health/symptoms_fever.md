# Fever – Basic Knowledge

## When Fever Is Mild
- 37.5–38.4°C is usually mild.
- Hydration and rest help most individuals.

## Over-the-counter General Guidance
Common OTC fever reducers include acetaminophen or ibuprofen.
Always follow dosing instructions and avoid if contraindicated.

## Red Flags
Seek urgent care if:
- Fever > 40°C
- Difficulty breathing
- Confusion, severe headache
