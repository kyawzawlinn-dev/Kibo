# Pain Relievers

## Acetaminophen
- Type: Analgesic, Antipyretic
- Effect: Reduces pain and fever
- Typical Dose: 500–1000 mg every 4–6 hours
- Max Daily Dose: 4000 mg
- Notes: Avoid in severe liver disease

## Ibuprofen
- Type: NSAID
- Effect: Reduces pain, fever, inflammation
- Typical Dose: 200–400 mg every 4–6 hours
- Max Daily Dose: 1200 mg (OTC), higher under doctor supervision
- Notes: Avoid if stomach ulcers or kidney problems; take with food

# Allergy Relief

## Loratadine
- Type: Antihistamine
- Effect: Reduces allergy symptoms (sneezing, runny nose, itching)
- Typical Dose: 10 mg once daily
- Max Daily Dose: 10 mg
- Notes: Non-drowsy; safe for adults and children >2 years

## Cetirizine
- Type: Antihistamine
- Effect: Relieves hay fever, hives
- Typical Dose: 10 mg once daily
- Max Daily Dose: 10 mg
- Notes: May cause mild drowsiness

# Gastrointestinal Relief

## Loperamide
- Type: Anti-diarrheal
- Effect: Controls diarrhea
- Typical Dose: 4 mg initially, then 2 mg after each loose stool
- Max Daily Dose: 16 mg
- Notes: Not for use in children <6 years or bloody diarrhea; consult doctor if symptoms persist

## Bismuth Subsalicylate
- Type: Anti-diarrheal, anti-nausea
- Effect: Relieves diarrhea, nausea, upset stomach
- Typical Dose: 525 mg every 30–60 min as needed
- Max Daily Dose: 4200 mg
- Notes: Can cause dark stool; avoid in children with viral infections

## Antacids (e.g., Calcium carbonate)
- Type: Antacid
- Effect: Relieves heartburn, indigestion
- Typical Dose: 500–1000 mg as needed
- Max Daily Dose: Follow package instructions
- Notes: Take after meals; separate from other medications

# Nausea / Vomiting

## Dimenhydrinate
- Type: Anti-emetic, antihistamine
- Effect: Reduces nausea, motion sickness
- Typical Dose: 50–100 mg every 4–6 hours
- Max Daily Dose: 400 mg
- Notes: May cause drowsiness; avoid alcohol

## Meclizine
- Type: Anti-emetic
- Effect: Motion sickness, vertigo
- Typical Dose: 25–50 mg 1 hour before activity
- Max Daily Dose: 50 mg per day
- Notes: Less sedating than dimenhydrinate
