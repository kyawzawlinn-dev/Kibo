# Diabetes – General Information

## Blood Sugar Ranges
- Normal fasting: 70–99 mg/dL
- Prediabetes: 100–125 mg/dL
- Diabetes: ≥ 126 mg/dL

## Common Symptoms
- Increased thirst
- Frequent urination
- Blurry vision
- Fatigue

## Red Flags
Seek immediate help if:
- Blood glucose > 300 mg/dL with symptoms
- Rapid breathing
- Confusion or inability to stay awake
