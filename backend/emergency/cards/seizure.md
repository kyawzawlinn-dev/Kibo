# Seizure / convulsion
keywords: seizure, convulsion, convulsing, having a fit, epileptic

1. Stay calm and note the time the seizure started.
2. Protect them from injury: move hard objects away and put something soft under their head.
3. Do NOT hold them down and do NOT put anything in their mouth.
4. When the jerking stops, roll them onto their side and tilt the head back to keep the airway open.
5. Stay with them until they are fully awake; speak calmly and explain what happened.

Get emergency help if: the seizure lasts more than 5 minutes, another one follows, they are injured, pregnant, or diabetic, it happened in water, or it is their first seizure.
