# Unconscious person
keywords: unconscious, passed out, fainted, not waking up, not breathing, unresponsive

1. Check for a response: speak loudly and tap their shoulders.
2. If no response, check breathing: watch the chest, listen and feel for breath for up to 10 seconds.
3. If breathing normally: roll them onto their side (recovery position), tilt the head back to keep the airway open, and watch them until help arrives.
4. If NOT breathing normally: start CPR — push hard and fast in the centre of the chest, about 2 pushes per second. Do not stop until help arrives or they start breathing.
5. Send someone for help immediately.

If someone fainted briefly and woke up: have them lie down with legs raised and rest. Get medical advice if it happens again, they are pregnant, or they have a heart condition.
