# Severe bleeding
keywords: severe bleeding, bleeding heavily, bleeding a lot, bleeding won't stop, won't stop bleeding, deep cut

1. Have the person sit or lie down.
2. Press firmly on the wound with a clean cloth or bandage. Keep pressing — do not lift it to check.
3. If blood soaks through, add more cloth on top; do not remove the first layer.
4. Raise the injured limb above heart level if possible.
5. Keep pressure on until the bleeding stops or help arrives.
6. If an object is stuck in the wound, do NOT pull it out — press around it instead.
7. Watch for pale skin, cold sweat, or confusion (shock): lay the person flat, raise their legs, and keep them warm.

Severe bleeding needs professional care — get help now.
