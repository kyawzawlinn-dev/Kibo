# Snakebite
keywords: snakebite, snake bite, snake bit, bitten by a snake

1. Move the person away from the snake. Do not try to catch or kill it.
2. Keep the person calm and as still as possible — movement spreads venom faster.
3. Remove rings, watches, and tight clothing near the bite before swelling starts.
4. Keep the bitten limb still and below heart level. Splint it if you can.
5. Do NOT cut the wound, suck out venom, apply ice, or tie a tight tourniquet.
6. Note the time of the bite and the snake's appearance if you saw it safely.
7. Get to medical care as fast as possible — antivenom is the only effective treatment for venomous bites.
